# Getting Started

**Project Sentinel is currently in closed pre-alpha.**

We are working directly with a small number of GovCon firms to validate the engine against live solicitations. This is not a beta program with a waitlist. It is a working engagement with a narrow group of operators who have agreed to run real RFPs through the system and give honest feedback on the output.

If you are here, you are either evaluating whether to request access or someone at Invariant pointed you here directly.

---

## What a Pilot Engagement Looks Like

We keep it simple at this stage:

- You bring **1–3 active or recently closed solicitations**
- We run them through the full Sentinel pipeline
- You receive the Bid Cost Report and Excel baseline for each
- You tell us where the output is right, where it is wrong, and what is missing

No integration work on your end. No new software to install. No changes to your existing proposal process.

---

## What We Ask For Upfront

Before the first run, we will ask for:

- A sample solicitation from your pipeline — ideally one you have already priced manually so we can benchmark the output
- Your internal labor category list and loaded rates
- A brief on your market focus — NAICS codes, set-aside certifications, and typical contract value range

That is it for now. As the platform matures, the onboarding process will become more structured. At this stage, we move fast and keep the overhead low.
